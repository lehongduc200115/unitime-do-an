Welcome to our University Timetabling Engine!

Our timetabling engine is designed to help you plan and organize your schedule for the upcoming academic year. With this tool, you can easily create a personalized timetable that meets your unique needs and preferences.

Getting Started

  1. Select your university or college from the list of institutions provided.
  2. Choose your courses for the upcoming semester or academic year.
  3. Customize your schedule by selecting the days and times that work best for you.
  4. Generate your personalized timetable and save it for future reference.
